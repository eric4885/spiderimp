Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026 · Applies to spiderimp.com (SpiderImp)

Summary

SpiderImp is a free browser Spider Solitaire game. You do not need an account or download. Gameplay progress (scores, statistics, settings, and unfinished games) is stored locally in your browser on your device. We do not require you to submit a name, email, or password to play.

Information stored on your device

Using browser storage (such as localStorage), the game may keep:

  • Best scores and move/time results by difficulty
  • Statistics (games played, wins, averages) by difficulty
  • Daily Challenge personal bests for the current day and mode
  • Volume / mute preference and resume state for an unfinished deal

Clearing site data for spiderimp.com in your browser settings removes this information. It is not synced to a SpiderImp account because no account is created.

Server logs and hosting

Like most websites, our hosting and CDN provider (for example Cloudflare) may process standard technical data such as IP address, browser type, and request URLs to deliver pages, maintain security, and measure availability. We use this for operations—not to build a player profile for gameplay.

Advertising and analytics

If we enable third-party advertising (such as Google AdSense) or analytics in the future, those partners may use cookies or similar technologies according to their own policies. You can control cookies through your browser settings. This page will be updated when such services are added.

Children

The game is a general-audience card puzzle. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Parents can clear browser site data at any time.

External links

If this site links to other websites, their privacy practices are their own. Review their policies before providing personal information there.

Changes

We may update this policy as the site evolves. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of spiderimp.com after updates means you accept the revised policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy: use the contact method published on spiderimp.com when available, or reach out via the channel you used to find SpiderImp (for example the site footer or project page).
