Contact Us

Feedback, bug reports, and questions about free Spider Solitaire on spiderimp.com.

Email

Write to support@spiderimp.com. Please include your browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Safari), device (PC / Mac / phone), and a short description of what happened. Screenshots help for layout or display bugs.

What we can help with

  • Game rules questions and how Daily Challenge works
  • Display, sound, or save/resume issues
  • Privacy or Terms questions
  • Partnership or content-use requests

Before you write

Many answers are already in How to Play, the in-game FAQ, and Privacy. Scores live only in your browser—clearing site data for spiderimp.com resets them.

Response time

We aim to reply when we can. SpiderImp is a small free project, so please allow a few days. Urgent security reports are welcome at the same address with “Security” in the subject line.
