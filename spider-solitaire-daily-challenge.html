Spider Solitaire Daily Challenge

Every calendar day, SpiderImp deals the same layout for each difficulty so you can race yourself—and compare notes with friends on the same puzzle.

How the Daily Challenge works

  • From the main menu, choose 1 / 2 / 4 suits, then tap Daily Challenge.
  • That day’s starting deal is fixed for that mode—everyone gets the same cards.
  • Your best finish for the day (fewest moves, then fastest time) is saved on your device.
  • Restart keeps today’s deal so you can retry. Tomorrow brings a new seed.
  • After you clear today’s puzzle, the menu can show that you already beat the day for that mode.

Why play Daily?

Random deals are great for practice; Daily turns Spider into a short daily habit: one puzzle, clear goals, and a fair comparison when you share a score. Use Copy Score after a win to paste moves and time into a message.

Tips for beating today’s deal

  • Reveal face-down cards early; empty columns are your rearranging tools.
  • Prefer same-suit builds before temporary mixed stacks.
  • Delay dealing from the stock until tableau moves run dry.
  • Use Hint when stuck, Undo after a bad deal, and Pause (P) if you need a break.
