About SpiderImp

SpiderImp is a free browser Spider Solitaire site at spiderimp.com—built for quick play, clear rules, and a classic feel without an app store download.

What you get

  • 1 / 2 / 4 suit difficulty modes
  • Daily Challenge with the same deal for everyone each day
  • Undo, Hint, Pause, Restart, and volume control
  • Best Scores and Statistics stored locally on your device
  • Works on desktop and mobile browsers—no signup required

Who it’s for

Players in the United States, United Kingdom, Canada, Australia, and worldwide who want a calm, classic solitaire session—at home, on a break, or on the go.
